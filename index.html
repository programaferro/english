Q&A And The Origins Of The Easter Egg

Join us next week as we continue our conclusion of Our Resurrection Through The Passover Of The Jews. We will conclude the study, as well as answer some questions and statements from our listeners.

Can the gospel accounts of His resurrection be trusted because they have different descriptions of what happened that day? What is the source for coloring the Easter eggs in blood and where did the concept of the Easter eggs come from?
